Support available for council tax

How do I view my council tax account online?

Go to Council tax to register or manage your account online. There you can:

  • set up a direct debit
  • check your account balance
  • check your next payment date
  • change your address
  • download a cope of your bill
  • apply for an exemption or discount and more
